## 1. Brand Identity & Positioning

### Positioning Statement
> For modern professionals, parents, and students seeking stress relief and holistic wellness, **Sadhana** is the premium wellness platform that brings authentic, integrated Indian yoga, meditation, and breathwork (Pranayama) into simple daily routines. Unlike generic fitness apps or secular mindfulness trackers, our platform connects ancient Eastern wisdom with modern science to create a complete daily ritual (*Sadhana*) for mind and body.

### Brand Personality Archetypes
*   **Primary Archetype: The Sage (The Guide)**
    *   *Characteristics:* Knowledgeable, authentic, evidence-based, clear, thoughtful.
    *   *Expression:* We don't just instruct; we explain the *why*, referencing traditional philosophy and modern neuroscience.
*   **Secondary Archetype: The Caregiver (The Restorer)**
    *   *Characteristics:* Warm, welcoming, non-judgmental, calming.
    *   *Expression:* Soft, encouraging tones. Visual design that feels like a quiet sanctuary.

### Brand Voice and Tone Guidelines
*   **Authentic, not esoteric:** We use traditional Sanskrit terms (like *Pranayama*, *Asana*, *Sattva*) but always define them simply. We avoid mystical jargon and explain benefits in clear, modern terms.
*   **Premium & Calm:** We speak with quiet confidence. No loud marketing exclamation points. Our tone is deliberate, grounded, and steady.
*   **Encouraging & Practical:** We understand modern schedules. We celebrate small accomplishments (e.g., "A 5-minute breathwork session is better than no session"). We never use guilt-inducing language.

## 2. Naming Candidates (Sanskrit Roots)

We evaluate six naming candidates based on meaning, global pronunciation, app store searchability, and brand potential.

### Candidate 1: **Sadhana**
*   **STATUS: APPROVED & SELECTED BRAND NAME**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Sādhana* (साधन)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "Dedicated practice", "spiritual practice", or "a means of accomplishing something". It represents a daily commitment to self-realization.
*   **Brand Vibe:** Structured, intentional, premium. It reframes a "daily workout" as a sacred ritual of self-care.
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `sadhana.app`, `sadhana.life`, `yoursadhana.com`
    *   *App Store Title:* `Sadhana: Authentic Daily Yoga`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Easy. *Sah-duh-nuh*.
*   **Critique:** High alignment with the "integrated daily routine" feature. Feels grounded and serious.

### Candidate 2: **Prana**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Prāṇa* (प्राण)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "Life force", "vital energy", or "breath". It is the energy that flows through all living things.
*   **Brand Vibe:** Energizing, active, vital. Fits an app with a strong focus on breathwork (Pranayama).
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `pranadaily.com`, `findprana.app`, `pranawellness.life`
    *   *App Store Title:* `Prana: Breathwork & Meditation`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Very easy. *Prah-nuh*.
*   **Critique:** Very punchy and easy to remember. Excellent for global marketing.

### Candidate 3: **Sattva**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Sattva* (सत्त्व)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "Purity", "harmony", "balance", "truth", or "light". It represents the quality of balance and peace of mind.
*   **Brand Vibe:** Calm, elegant, restorative. Speaks to stress-relief and mental clarity.
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `sattva.app` (likely taken, check alternatives like `sattvalife.app`, `sattvadaily.com`)
    *   *App Store Title:* `Sattva: Mind-Body Wellness`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Easy. *Sat-vuh*.
*   **Critique:** Feels extremely premium and peaceful. Sounds like a luxury brand.

### Candidate 4: **Dhyana**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Dhyāna* (ध्यान)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "Meditation", "contemplation", or "profound reflection". The etymological root of *Zen* and *Chan*.
*   **Brand Vibe:** Contemplative, deep, serene. Focuses heavily on the mental/meditative aspect.
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `dhyana.app`, `dhyanawellness.com`
    *   *App Store Title:* `Dhyana: Authentic Meditation`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Moderate. *Dhyah-nuh* (the "dh" sound can be tricky for Westerners, often simplified to *Dee-ah-nuh*).
*   **Critique:** Strong association with meditation. Very traditional.

### Candidate 5: **Yuj**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Yuj* (युज्)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "To yoke", "unite", "connect", or "join". This is the root verb of the word *Yoga*.
*   **Brand Vibe:** Minimalist, tech-forward, connective.
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `yuj.app`, `yujwellness.com`
    *   *App Store Title:* `Yuj: Yoga & Breathwork`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Easy but unfamiliar. *Yooj* (rhymes with huge).
*   **Critique:** Bold and modern. Great visual brand potential (short 3-letter logo).

### Candidate 6: **Ananda**
*   **Sanskrit Root:** *Ānanda* (आनन्द)
*   **Literal Meaning:** "Divine bliss", "spiritual joy", or "extreme happiness".
*   **Brand Vibe:** Uplifting, warm, positive. Emphasizes the emotional benefit of wellness.
*   **ASO & Domain Strategy:**
    *   *Domain options:* `anandalife.app`, `findananda.com`
    *   *App Store Title:* `Ananda: Yoga & Peace`
*   **Pronunciation (Global):** Very easy. *Ah-nuhn-duh*.
*   **Critique:** Extremely friendly and inviting. Excellent emotional appeal.
